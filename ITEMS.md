> **Total cost: 450g** for all 3. Combined with Arcane Tome (-1 session), saves up to **2 sessions per degree** (8 instead of 10). At 25g/session for top-tier degrees, that's **50g saved per degree**.

## Academy (Scholar)

*"Knowledge is the truest treasure."*

### Degree Courses

Each degree requires **10 sessions** (reduced by Extra Credit items), **6 hours per session**. Education points: **+9 per degree completed** (99 max across all 11 degrees). Graduation gives **+5 Happiness, +5 Dependability**.

#### Starting Degrees (No Prerequisites)

| Degree | Cost/Session | Total Cost | Unlocks Jobs |
|--------|-------------|-----------|--------------|
| Trade Guild Certificate | 5g | 50g | Shop Clerk, Market Vendor, Apprentice Smith, Tavern Chef |
| Junior Academy Diploma | 5g | 50g | Scribe, Library Assistant, Bank Teller |

#### Trade Guild Path

| Degree | Cost/Session | Total Cost | Prerequisites | Unlocks Jobs |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| Arcane Studies Certificate | 8g | 80g | Trade Guild | Enchantment Assistant, Scroll Copier |
| Combat Training Certificate | 8g | 80g | Trade Guild | City Guard, Caravan Guard, Journeyman Smith |
| Master Combat Degree | 12g | 120g | Combat Training | Arena Fighter, Weapons Instructor, Master Smith, Forge Manager |

#### Junior Academy Path

| Degree | Cost/Session | Total Cost | Prerequisites | Unlocks Jobs |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| Scholar Degree | 10g | 100g | Junior Academy | Teacher |
| Commerce Degree | 10g | 100g | Junior Academy | Guild Accountant, Shop Manager, Tavern Manager |
| Advanced Scholar Degree | 15g | 150g | Scholar | Senior Teacher, Researcher |
| Sage Studies Certificate | 20g | 200g | Advanced Scholar | Academy Lecturer |
| Loremaster Degree | 25g | 250g | Sage Studies | **Sage**, Court Advisor |

#### Combination Degree

| Degree | Cost/Session | Total Cost | Prerequisites | Unlocks Jobs |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| Alchemy Degree | 15g | 150g | Arcane Studies + Junior Academy | Alchemist, Potion Brewer |

> **Full education cost** (all 11 degrees, base prices): **1,330g** total + **660 hours** (110 sessions x 6h). With Extra Credit items (-2 sessions each), reduced to **88 sessions = 528 hours** and savings of ~220g.

## Item Properties Reference

### Durability & Theft

| Property | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **Durable** | Stored at your dwelling, persists between turns |
| **Stealable** | Shadowfingers can steal if you live in The Slums |
| **Unstealable** | Safe from robbery (Scholar texts, Dragon Scale Shield) |
| **Appliance** | Can break, needs repair. Break chance: 1/51 (enchanter) or 1/36 (market) |
| **Consumable** | Used immediately on purchase (food, potions) |

### Appliance Break Conditions

- Only triggers when player has **>500g** total
- Enchanter-sourced: **1/51** (~2%) break chance per turn
- Market/Fence-sourced: **1/36** (~2.8%) break chance per turn
- Repair cost: Random between **5-25%** of original purchase price

## Economy Notes

### Price Modifier

All prices in this document are **base prices**. The actual price is:

```
Actual Price = Base Price × priceModifier
```

The economy fluctuates weekly with `priceModifier` typically ranging from **0.8x to 1.3x**. This affects:
- All shop item prices
- Education session costs
- Housing rent (locked at move-in rate)
- Healing services
- Stock prices (volatile stocks)
